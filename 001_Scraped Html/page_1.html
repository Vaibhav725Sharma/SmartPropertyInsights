    Points to Consider

    What's great here!

    • Palam Vihar, a mid-segment locality, offers a majority of 3 BHK and 4 BHK builder floors and independent houses

    • Rajendra Park Road, Major Sushil Aima Marg and Rezang La Marg are the major arterial roads in the area

    • NH-48 and the partially operational Dwarka Expressway, accessible at just 3-6 km, provide widespread connectivity

    • Udyog Vihar, home to several auto, chemical, pharma and PVC industries, is about 6 km from the area

    • DLF Cyber City, a prominent technology hub in Gurgaon, is reachable at 8 km, while Aerocity is about 14 km away

    • Ansal API and Bestech Group are the prominent builders offering a significant number of residential units here

    • Preferred by tenants owing to its sound connectivity and proximity to several employment hubs

    • Several schools, such as DPS, Red Roses Public and Government Middle schools, are within the locality

    • The Gurgaon Railway Station, situated on the Delhi-Jaipur Line, is about 5 km away via Chauma Road

    • Dotted by the Park Hospital, Jai Hind Hospital and Manipal Hospital, which strengthen the medical infrastructure

    • Sector 23 Market, H Block Market and Ambience Mall are the famous shopping hubs within a 7 km radius of the locale

    • NEMA and RWA parks, among several other block parks, ensure ample greenery in and around the locality

    • Frequent flyers enjoy the proximity to the Indira Gandhi International Airport, which is just 13 km away via NH 344M

    What needs attention!

    • Some areas of Palam Vihar witness waterlogging after heavy downpours

    • Haphazard parking and encroachment by street vendors often lead to traffic congestion in Palam Vihar

    • Moulsari Avenue, the nearest metro station to Palam Vihar, is as far as 8 km via Major Sushil Aima Marg
